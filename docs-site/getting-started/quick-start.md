# Quick Start

Let's create your first diagram. This should take about five minutes.

## Create a New Document

1. **Launch Diagrammer** — Open the application. You'll see the Document Browser with your recent documents.

2. **Create a new document** — Click the **+ New Document** button or press `Ctrl+N` (`Cmd+N` on Mac).

3. **Name your document** — Enter a name for your diagram and click **Create**.

Your document opens with an infinite canvas ready for drawing.

## Add Your First Shapes

1. **Select the Rectangle tool** — Press `R` or click Rectangle in the toolbar.

2. **Draw a rectangle** — Click and drag on the canvas. Release to finish.

3. **Add more shapes** — Try an **Ellipse** (`O`) and a **Text** box (`T`).

::: tip
Hold **Shift** while drawing to constrain proportions — you'll get a perfect square or circle.
:::

## Connect Your Shapes

1. **Select the Connector tool** — Press `C` or click the connector icon in the toolbar.

2. **Connect two shapes** — Hover over a shape to see its connection points (small circles on the edges). Click one, then click a connection point on another shape.

3. **Watch the auto-routing** — Connectors automatically find the best path between shapes and update when you move things around.

## Edit and Style

- **Double-click** a shape to edit its text
- **Click** a shape to select it, then use the **Property Panel** on the right to change colors, borders, and fonts
- **Shift+Click** to select multiple shapes
- Press `Delete` to remove selected shapes

## Navigate the Canvas

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Pan | Middle-click + drag, or hold Space + drag |
| Zoom | Scroll wheel (zooms toward your cursor) |
| Reset zoom | Press `0` |
| Fit everything in view | Press `1` |
| WASD navigation | `W`/`A`/`S`/`D` keys pan like a game |

## Save and Export

Diagrammer **auto-saves** your work, so you won't lose anything. You can also:

- Press `Ctrl+S` to save immediately
- Use **Export** to save as PNG, SVG, PDF, or JSON
- Click the document name in the toolbar to rename it
